The Greenlandic band Sume (active primarily in the 1970s) released the following albums:

Sumut (1973) — The title means "Where to?" in Greenlandic. This was their debut album and historically significant as the first rock album sung in Greenlandic.

Sume (1974) — Self-titled second album.

Sume 3 (1977) — Their third album, often simply referred to as Sume 3.

Sume 4 (1979) — Their fourth and final album from their original run.
